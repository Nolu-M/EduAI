Energy

Energy is the ability to do work. It comes in various forms, including kinetic energy, potential energy, thermal energy, electrical energy, and more. Understanding energy and its transformations is essential in physics.

Motion

Motion refers to the change in position of an object over time. It can be described in terms of displacement, distance, velocity, acceleration, and time. The study of motion is fundamental in physics.

Waves

Waves are disturbances that transfer energy from one place to another. They can be mechanical, like sound waves, or electromagnetic, like light waves. Understanding waves and their properties is crucial in various fields of science.
